about me

Six years ago, I started making things with code after a brief affair with designing for print when I moved to Brooklyn. I've never looked back and enjoy every step of the journey that continues to evolve. Most of the work I've done is through an agency where the type of work and pace can vary from project to project. I have been fortunate to work with many lovely people, clients, and tools to create immersive experiences that meet our collective goals. I love to learn, build, and create neat things.

about me
