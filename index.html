about me

Six years ago, I started making things with code after a brief affair with designing for print when I moved to Brooklyn. I've never looked back and enjoy every step of the journey that continues to evolve. Most of the work I've done is through an agency where the type of work and pace can vary from project to project. I have been fortunate to work with many lovely people, clients, and tools to create immersive experiences that meet our collective goals. I love to learn, build, and create neat things.

The New York Review of Books

In 2019 I worked with Athletics to help bring a richer experience to NYRB's forty plus years of archival content. The site was built with WordPress, Timber, Twig, ACF, React, Bootstrap, Javascript, and is now hosted on WordPress VIP. The NYRB site won a people's choice webby in 2020.

TURF

In 2022 I worked with the Athletics team to switch from WordPress to using Sanity.io and bring their new brand identity to life. This project uses React, Next.js, Sanity.io, and other third party tools to bring a rich experience and understanding of TURF's beautifully made felt acoustic panelling.
